Is this only for correcting wrong candidate details?

No. The main value is saving recruiter time after candidate data is collected: duplicate removal, formatting, missing-field checks, sheet merging, and calling/client-ready workbook preparation.

No. I only work with Excel/CSV files shared by your team. No passwords, logins, or ATS access are required.

Can we send anonymized data?

Yes. For a sample check, candidate names and contact details can be removed or masked.
